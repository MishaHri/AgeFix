Mission

I'm Abby to my friends and Gabi to my family, a passionate aesthetician nurse practitioner born in Romania. With a solid background as a certified nurse since 2009, actively practicing in neurology, I've always been fascinated by skin health. This fascination led me to follow my passion and dedicate my entire focus to beauty and the science behind it. At AgeFix, I've made it my mission to help individuals feel more confident by enhancing their beauty through safe and effective Botox and face booster treatments. My commitment is to provide specialized treatments with expertise and compassion, unlocking and maintaining the timeless beauty within each client. The name "AgeFix" embodies our core values of fixing and maintaining, aiming to bring out the best version of yourself. Our logo encapsulates our philosophy: "Where time meets beauty." AgeFix is not just about injectables; we place a strong emphasis on well-being and take a holistic approach to beauty. We believe in nurturing your inner radiance alongside enhancing your external beauty. AgeFix is dedicated to unleashing your timeless beauty, helping you achieve your desired results while preserving your natural essence. Join us on this transformative journey where we strive to bring out the inherent, ageless beauty within you.

Radiantly yours,
